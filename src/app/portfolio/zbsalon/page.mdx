## Overview

Zobeida's Hair Salon needed a website that looked as professional as the work being done inside the salon. I built a polished, design-forward marketing site from scratch — services, pricing, gallery, and contact — with a focus on local SEO so clients in Kelowna can actually find her.

The site is a fully static Next.js build, which means it loads fast, works perfectly on mobile, and costs almost nothing to host. Every page is built around Zobeida's real services and her own photography.
